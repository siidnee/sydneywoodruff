Purpose
Welcome to the Beyoncé Lemonade fanpage - your main source for information on the album and artist. This site is to inform you on the 2016 album, providing you with up to date news about the project.
The Album
Lemonade, the visual album, was released April 23, 2016 without any promotion. Directly after, the "Formation Tour" successfully began, with fans already knowing the lyrics. The album gives us a small peek into Beyonce's life, including infidelity and restoration, as well as empowering women to stand up and have pride in themselves. The message throughout poems and lyrics within encourage independence and confidence in oneself. Several of
performances of the album also encourage unity and black empowerment.
