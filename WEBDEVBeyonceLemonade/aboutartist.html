About Artist
Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter is a Houston Texas born American singer, songwriter, and actress. As a child she competed in numerous competitions and pagents, all while singing in the star's first singing group, Girls Tyme. Later in the 90's, she rose to fame while singing as lead in the R&B group, Destiny's Child. This group grew to become one of the best selling girl groups of all time. In 2006, Beyoncé decided to go solo, starting with her debut album, Dangerously in Love, and also starring in "Austin Powers in Goldmember" movie. This was just the start of the singers journey through her career. Throughout the years she has earned 22 grammy awards, with 62 nominations, also completing 6 album tours.
